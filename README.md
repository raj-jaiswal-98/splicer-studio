# 🎨 Poster Splicer Studio

A high-performance, Neo-Brutalist design suite for creating massive multi-page posters. Splice any image into a grid of printable pages with pixel-perfect accuracy.

## 🚀 Key Features

### 🖼️ Multi-Layer Canvas Compositor
*   **Floating Image Layers**: Add multiple images to your canvas. Resize, rotate, and move them with ease.
*   **Opacity & Blending**: Adjust transparency for sophisticated "photo chaining" and layering effects.
*   **Set as Background**: Instantly convert any floating layer into a full-grid background "cover".

### 📂 Multi-Project Tabs
*   **Tabbed Workflow**: Work on multiple poster designs simultaneously.
*   **Auto-Persistence**: Your projects are automatically saved locally in your browser.
*   **Rename & Manage**: Easily keep track of different design iterations.

### 📐 Precision Slicing Engine
*   **Intelligent Grids**: Split images into any grid size (e.g., 2x2, 3x3, up to 10x10).
*   **Paper Presets**: Support for A3, A4, A5, Letter, Legal, and Tabloid sizes at 300 DPI.
*   **Bleed Margins**: Customizable overlap (0-20mm) to ensure seamless taping after printing.
*   **Fit Modes**: 'Cover', 'Contain', 'Stretch', and 'Blur Background' options.

### ✍️ Dynamic Text Overlays
*   **Brutalist Typography**: Add bold, high-contrast text to your designs.
*   **Drag-and-Drop Editing**: Position text precisely on the virtual poster coordinates.

### 🖨️ Professional Export & Printing
*   **Print Direct**: Instant high-resolution print dialog trigger.
*   **PDF Export**: Generates a single, high-fidelity PDF with one page per slice.
*   **ZIP Export**: Download all individual slices as high-quality JPEGs in a single bundle.
*   **Full Image Save**: Export your entire composition as a single high-res file.

## 🛠️ Technology Stack
*   **React + Vite**: Ultra-fast HMR and performance.
*   **Canvas API**: Hardware-accelerated image processing and slicing.
*   **jsPDF & JSZip**: Client-side asset generation.
*   **Lucide React**: Clean, consistent iconography.
*   **Vanilla CSS**: Custom Neo-Brutalist design system.

## 🎨 Design Philosophy
The app follows a **Neo-Brutalist** aesthetic:
*   High-contrast colors (Pink, Yellow, Cyan).
*   Thick, bold black borders.
*   Strong typography.
*   Functional, honest, and fast UI.
